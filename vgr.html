~Video Game Recommendations~

No Man's Sky

Amazing exploration game with a vast universe to discover. ~18 quintillion planets! *Fair Warning: May run badly on older systems.*

Price: Can be found on sale for ~$20, usually $60

Genre: Exploration, Survival, Sci-Fi

Platform: PC, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S

rating: 8/10, slight hiccups, 25-ish FPS but I have a potato laptop.

Terraria

2D sandbox adventure game with mining, crafting, and exploration elements.

Price: various, around $10 to $20

Genre: Sandbox, Adventure, Mining

Platform: PC, Mac, Linux, Xbox One, PlayStation 4, Nintendo Switch

rating: 10/10. no notes. Runs on any system, SOO many bosses. always fun even when you die on expert mode skeletron for the 4,403rd time.

Trailmakers

Physics-based vehicle building and racing game with a focus on creativity and destruction.

Price: various, usually $25, on sale for $7.50 sometimes

Genre: Physics, Racing, Building

Platform: PC, Mac, Linux

rating: 8/10. havn't played it much, but from the little I played, it seems like a fun physics-based game. the controls can be a bit wonky, so watch some tutorials first.

Mech Arena

Fast-paced multiplayer arena combat game with mech customization and intense battles.

Price: free, pay to win though.

Genre: Action, Shooter, Multiplayer

Platform: PC

rating: 6/10. Fun for competitive multiplayer battles, but can be a bit repetitive after a while. *Fair Warning: Extremely Pay to Win, Past rank III, you will want to uninstall, but keep going because it gets better.*

Grimhook

fast paced parkour challenge with a unique hook-based movement system.

Price: free

Genre: Parkour, Challenge

Platform: PC

rating: 8/10. fun, but extremely short. takes less than 30 minutes to beat.

Hollow Knight

Beautiful 2D metroidvania game with a dark, atmospheric world to explore.

Price: Various, around $15, but can be on sale.

Genre: Metroidvania, Adventure, Platformer

Platform: PC, Mac, Linux, Nintendo Switch

rating: 9/10. A masterpiece of game design and atmosphere. Highly recommended for fans of challenging platformers. also the bosses are amazing.

Hollow Knight Silksong

Beautiful 2D metroidvania sequel with a bright world to explore.

Price: Various, around $15, but can be on sale.

Genre: Metroidvania, Adventure, Platformer

Platform: PC, Mac, Linux, Nintendo Switch

rating: 9.5/10. A masterpiece of game design and atmosphere. Highly recommended for fans of challenging platformers. also the bosses are amazing but much harder than the original.
